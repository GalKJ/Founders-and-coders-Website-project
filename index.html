Welcome to my website.

Hi! My name is Gareth and I enjoy creating experiences that are shared with other humans, interactions that are urgent, humorous or helpful. I have worked in live theatre as an actor and a lighting technician for 20 years always with an interest in how technology can enrich the stories we tell and make them more dynamic and accessible. The web is a space that enables us to share stories worldwide and I want to master the tools that will enable me to be an active voice in this conversation and an individual able to elevate others voices too.üôå
